Our Mission

Our goal is to support students in the Madison area in their pursuit of STEM by providing resources, support and funding for Science clubs at their schools! We want every student to have those experiences that inspire them to pursue STEM careers. Find out more about what we do or how to support us today!

Science Olympiad

Our main mission is supporting Science Olympiad Teams in the Madison Area. Science Olympiad is a team based STEM competition that allows schools to engage teams of up to 15 students in a sort of “field day” format competition. We help connect schools with resources and funding to run their teams and make this competition more accessible. We proudly sponsor the only invitational in the Madison Area, the Badger Invitational!

Sounds of Music Chamber Group

We operate and support a small chamber orchestra that performs concerts to benefit our organization and to provide music to the Madison communities! Many students we support are not only passionate about STEM but also are talented musicians! To join or get more info, contact us.
