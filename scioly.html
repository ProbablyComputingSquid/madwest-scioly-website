Science Olympiad

Our main mission is supporting Science Olympiad Teams in the Madison Area. Science Olympiad is a team based STEM competition that allows schools to engage teams of up to 15 students in a sort of “field day” format competition. We help connect schools with resources and funding to run their teams and make this competition more accessible. We proudly sponsor the only invitational in the Madison Area, the Badger Invitational!
