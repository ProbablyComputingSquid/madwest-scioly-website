Quality of Competition

Our tournament is a nice local madison tournament, having hosted 12-ish teams from one state at our 2024 tournament. These included 2 teams who placed in the 2024 National Tournament.

Quality of Events

We have a variety of events, including all the events from the 2024 National Tournament, as well as some of our own events.

Awards & Results

We award medals or ribbons through eighth place for each event, and trophies to the best eight schools. A number of spirit awards and special prizes are also distributed. Additionally, we release raw scores, tests, and answer keys for all events to assist teams in better understanding and improving their performance.

Experienced Leadership

We have Kevin Bao.

Concessions and Hospitality

We run an inexpensive concessions stand for all participants and guests. We also provide a hospitality room for coaches and event supervisors.
